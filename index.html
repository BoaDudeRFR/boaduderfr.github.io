Usage of AI Generated works in your projects

Copyrightability of AI-generated works has been subject to vigorous debate. Thalmer vs. Perlmutter (see here) may be the seminal court case which sheds light on this issue. According to the analysis done by Baker Donelson law firm, "The Court denied certiorari on March 2, 2026," (see here) "concluding this particular litigation, though the broader legal landscape remains in flux." To avoid licensing issues, I would personally advise against using AI-generated works in your projects.

This list features asset repositories that allow you to download their contents free-of-charge. Most of the following resources should be free to use on your blog, youtube video, personal projects etc. See the license details on whether they can be used in a specific commercial or non-commercial context (for example, not all content can be freely used in video games). You should be able to use the items with a green background in any conext (I have checked to the best of my ability and these appear to be in the public domain. As always, check for yourself before importing them into your projects, whatever they may be.)
